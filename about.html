ABOUT ME
Hi there! Welcome to my website. I'm a technical product manager based out of beautiful Austin, Texas with 5+ years of experience in the software development lifecycle, ranging from QA engineering, project and program management, business analysis and more!


I picked up painting in 2020 amid the pandemic, and fell in love immediately. I specialize in abstract acrylic and mixed media paintings that elicit flow & feeling.

Fast-forward a few years, and I now have far too much art sitting around my house! I created this website to showcase my art, my writing, and my other interests, as well as make a few business connections along the way.
